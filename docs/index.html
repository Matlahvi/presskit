Description

The main character is Eidos, a soul on their way to reach the perfect city of Atlantis. Eidos must learn about themselves and the world in order to grow and become their own best version. To enter Atlantis, Eidos must pass a series of tests that are based on the seven liberal arts. They take form of various puzzles in a 3D environment told in minimal, low-poly art and greek patterns. It is an experience of growth and beauty.

History

Tahutahu was born as the union of a four women from across the globe who fell in love with the project of its founder, Andrea Sacchi. Almost all of us with previous experience in the industry and with much desire to start our own project. We are spread across Spain, the Czech Republic and Japan. With remote organization, we work hard to create Idearum. We finally met face to face during the third edition of Stugan accelerator progam in 2017. We got to show our progress at events such as Sweden Game Conference, Games Developer Conference, IndieMad and even at museums such as TCC Gallery in Chicago or the Children's Creativity Museum in San Francisco.

Features

  • Idearum is based on platonic myths and Greek philosophy. While learning about the world around them, Eidos will be able to solve different puzzles. This will give them access to the perfect city of Atlantis.
  • We base our decisions on three pillars: education, emotion and design. We believe games, as cultural products, have an impact on the players.
  • All the in-game music is done by Maggie Bigelow, who has 10 years of experience as a composer and contributes by creating the perfect atmosphere of Atlantis.
